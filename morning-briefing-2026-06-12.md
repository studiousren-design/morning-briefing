### TSM — Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company
TSMC reported **May 2026 revenue of NT$416.98 billion** — up 1.5% month-over-month and up **30.1% year-over-year**. Taiwan's total stock market capitalisation has now surpassed India's to become the fifth largest in the world, a milestone significantly driven by TSMC's rally this year.

**Markets under pressure from inflation and geopolitical tension**

The S&P 500 fell **1.6%** on Wednesday and the Nasdaq Composite dropped nearly **2%** — driven by a hotter-than-expected Producer Price Index reading. Headline wholesale prices climbed **1.1%**, rising at the fastest pace since late 2022 and reigniting concerns that inflation is proving sticky.
