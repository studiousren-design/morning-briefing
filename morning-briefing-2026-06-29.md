### Alphabet (GOOG) — MAJOR NEWS: Joining the Dow Jones Today
This is the headline story for GOOG holders today. **Alphabet officially joins the 30-stock Dow Jones Industrial Average this morning**, replacing Verizon Communications. S&P Dow Jones Indices announced this change on 23 June, and it takes effect at today's open. Because the Dow weights by share price rather than market capitalisation, Alphabet at approximately **$345 per share carries roughly seven times more daily influence** on the index than Verizon did at around $46. Institutional funds and exchange-traded funds that track the Dow are being forced to buy Alphabet shares to mirror the rebalance — this buying pressure could provide a short-term tailwind to the stock. Alphabet joins NVIDIA, Amazon, Apple, and Microsoft in the blue-chip index.
