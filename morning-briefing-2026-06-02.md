## 🚌 Section 1: Getting to Work — Traffic & Transport

Good morning! Let's start with how to get from South Woodford to Newham Collegiate Sixth Form Centre today — and I have some important news that could change your morning.

**There is a full-day Tube strike today.** The RMT union has called 24-hour strikes on both Tuesday 2 June and Thursday 4 June. This affects the London Underground network from midnight to 11:59 PM.

**Central Line impact:** There are partial closures running between White City and Liverpool Street. Since South Woodford sits on the Epping branch of the Central Line east of Liverpool Street, services into Central London will be severely disrupted. Trains are expected to start very late, with limited services before 6:30 AM and again after 9 PM.

**Today's train recommendation: DO NOT take the train via the Central Line.** Stick to your buses today.

**Bus Route Update — W13:** No specific disruptions reported for the W13 service between South Woodford and Wanstead at the time of this briefing. This part of your commute should be fine.

**Bus Route Update — 101:** Heads up here. Route 101 is currently diverting due to street works in the East Ham area. It is running via Barking Road and Ron Leighton Way instead of its usual path, and the Caledon Road stop on High Street North is temporarily not served. Use bus stop BS on Ron Leighton Way instead. This diversion is expected to remain in place until High Street North reopens, which is not expected until late September 2026.

**Alternative option:** The Elizabeth Line is NOT affected by the strike and will run normally — but it will be significantly busier than usual as people avoid the Tube. If you can get to Stratford station, you could consider the Elizabeth Line as part of a revised route.

**Summary:** Take the W13 to Wanstead as normal, then hop on the 101 toward East Ham — just be ready for the diversion around Caledon Road and follow signs for bus stop BS on Ron Leighton Way. Give yourself extra time, as buses will be busier given the Tube strike.
