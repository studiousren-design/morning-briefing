NVIDIA has had an enormous few days. At Computex in Taiwan, Chief Executive Jensen Huang announced:

- **RTX Spark superchip** — a new system-on-chip for Windows laptops, debuting on devices from Microsoft, Dell, HP, ASUS, Lenovo, and MSI.
