### The Iran Conflict — Escalating Geopolitical Risk

The main driver of Monday's selloff was a fresh escalation in the Iran conflict. The **United Arab Emirates activated its missile alert system for the first time** since the US-Iran ceasefire, after intercepting missiles fired from Iran. This development has reignited concerns about:

- **Strait of Hormuz disruption** — the waterway through which approximately 20% of global oil supply passes
- **Sustained oil price inflation** feeding into broader consumer prices
- **Stagflation risk** — higher oil prices simultaneously weighing on economic growth while pushing inflation higher
- **Stock market 'euphoria' disconnect** — strategists are warning that US equities are underpricing the economic risk from sustained high oil prices

Defence-related stocks — including L3Harris (LHXBR) — may benefit from increased military spending momentum in this environment.
