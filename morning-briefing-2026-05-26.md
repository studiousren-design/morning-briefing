The **Elizabeth line** is running normally, as its staff were never part of the RMT action. The **London Overground** is also running as usual.

**Recommendation: The train is clear to use today.** The Central line from South Woodford gives you a straightforward route towards Stratford and onward connections to East Ham. No known disruptions as of this briefing.

### Artificial Intelligence and Technology

**NVIDIA (NVDA)**
The big story from last week is still reverberating. NVIDIA reported its first-quarter fiscal year 2027 results on 20 May, posting record revenue of **$81.6 billion — an 85% year-on-year increase**. Data centre revenue nearly doubled to $75.2 billion, accounting for 92% of all sales. Adjusted earnings per share came in at $1.87, ahead of analyst expectations of $1.76. The company also announced an **$80 billion share buyback programme** and a small dividend increase. However, the stock dipped slightly following the report — a classic "buy the rumour, sell the news" reaction, given the stock had already risen nearly 14% in the run-up to earnings. As of Monday, NVDA was trading between approximately $214 and $221.
